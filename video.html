This was a group project where I worked with four of my fellow classmates. Our task was to create a video for high school seniors to give them some more information about Millersville University. When starting this assignment, we came up with a topic and then created a story board. The story board was used to plan every scene we were going to film. Once that was created, we went out to film the designated scenes. When we were finished filming, we edited the clips in Apple iMovie to get the final product, which is shown above.
