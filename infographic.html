Final Infographic

This goal of this project was to create a visual display, infographic, of a communication technology. I chose to have my topic as the polaroid camera. I chose this because I have always been curious about how they work and how they were created. In this infographic I explained important dates, how the system works, how to use, and the impacts it has had and will have on the photography world. After I did a brief sketch of what I wanted my final product to look like, I opened Adobe InDesign. This is where I created the final product shown on the left.
